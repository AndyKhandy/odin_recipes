Chocolate Pretzels

Description

The best deserts (chocolate and pretzels) combined! This will fill your sweet and savory tooth!

Ingredients

  • 24 circular pretzels
  • 24 milk chocolate candy kisses
  • 1 (1.69 ounce) package mini candy-coated chocolates

Steps

  1. Preveat the oven to 350 degrees
  2. Spred pretzels evenly onto baking sheets and place unwrapped candy kisses in the center of each pretzel
  3. Bake in the preheated oven until kisses melt, 1 to 2 minutes
  4. Remove from the oven, then gently press a candy-coated chocolate into the melted chocolate center of each pretzel
  5. Chill in the refrigerator until set!
